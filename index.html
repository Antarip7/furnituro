Drawing room

A drawing room is a room in a house where visitors may be entertained, and a historical term for what would now, in the United States, be called a living room.

kitchen room

A kitchen is a room or part of a room used for cooking and food preparation in a dwelling or in a commercial establishment.

Dinning room
